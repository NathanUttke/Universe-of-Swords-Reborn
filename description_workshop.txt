[h1]Universe of Swords[/h1]

[i]Universe of Swords Reborn[/i] is a port and a continuation of the original [i]Universe of Swords[/i] from tModLoader 1.3, its mod creator, [i]Gnom[/i], abandoned the mod but has endorsed this continuation.
This continuation aims to make the mod better by reworking, respriting and balancing the items throughout the game's progression.
