Universe of Swords Mod Reborn is a port of the Universe of Swords Mod from tModLoader 1.3, but rebalanced, resprited and aiming for quality over quantity.
This includes removing and reworking many boring swords, balancing many weapons throughout progression and reworking a lot of material and weapon recipes for the better.
The original mod creator, Gnom and Horrior, abandoned the mod but has endoesed this port and gave us creative freedom going forward.
