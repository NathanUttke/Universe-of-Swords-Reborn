Universe of Swords Reborn is a continuation of the original [i]Universe of Swords[/i] from tModLoader 1.3, its mod creator, [i]Gnome[/i], abandoned the mod but has endorsed this continuation.
This continuation has the goal to make the mod better by reworking, respriting and balancing the items throughout the game's progression.
